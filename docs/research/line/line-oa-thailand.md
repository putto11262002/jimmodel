# LINE Official Account (Thailand) - Business Tools Summary

Date: 2026-01-03
Scope: Tools available in LINE Official Account (LINE OA) for businesses, with Thailand as the primary operating region.

## Executive summary
- LINE OA provides messaging, promotions, loyalty, and basic customer management tools for businesses.
- In Thailand, product and order management is available via LINE MyShop, which connects to LINE OA and is used to sell on LINE SHOPPING.
- Advanced CRM or e-commerce features are typically handled through MyShop or external systems + the LINE Messaging API.

## Messaging and customer engagement tools
- Greeting message: automatic welcome message when a user adds the OA.
- Auto-response messages: automatic replies based on keywords or timing.
- 1:1 chat: direct customer support and sales conversations.
- Broadcast messages: send announcements to all followers or segments.

## Promotions and retention
- Coupons: create and distribute discounts and offers.
- Reward cards (points/loyalty): track and grant points for purchases or visits.

## Rich content and navigation
- Rich menu: persistent menu in chat for key actions and links.
- Rich messages / rich media: visual message formats to promote products.

## Customer management
- Basic customer management through 1:1 chat, tags/notes, and broadcast targeting.
- For advanced CRM segmentation and automation, use external systems or the Messaging API.

## Selling products and product management (Thailand)
- LINE MyShop provides storefront, product management, inventory, payment, and delivery settings.
- MyShop is designed to sell on LINE SHOPPING and integrates with LINE OA.
- Availability and feature details can vary by region; Thailand has dedicated MyShop support.

## Developer/automation options
- LINE Messaging API enables custom bots, automated replies, coupon distribution, rich menus, and integrations with internal systems.

## Notes on regional differences
- Features and pricing vary by country/region (Japan, Thailand, Taiwan, etc.).
- Always confirm current plan limits and feature availability in Thailand.
